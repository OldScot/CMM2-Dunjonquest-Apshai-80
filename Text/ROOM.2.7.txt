Level 2 - Room Seven
The north wall of the corridor is native granite,
while the others are masonry. The worn path leads
to a wooden door in the west wall from which
dangles a heavy iron lock, now unfastened. The
hinges of the door show signs of frequent use.
The scraping sound can still be heard faintly.
