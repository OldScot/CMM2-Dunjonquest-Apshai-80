Level 2 - Room Thirty-nine through Forty-two
Form a narrow passage. The doors to the north are of
iron and have small barred windows at eye level and
small slots at ground level. All are secured with
rusted iron slidebolts. The floor is spattered with
dark stains. The rattling of chains and a faint
moaning echo through the corndor.
