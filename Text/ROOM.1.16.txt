Level 1 - Room Sixteen
Here you find the source of the roaring sound. A stream
flows into the chamber from the south, across the large
water wheel, and plummets into a pit in the floor of
the cave. The bottom of the pit cannot be seen, but
experiments will indicate that it is a very long drop.
