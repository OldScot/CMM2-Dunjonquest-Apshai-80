Level 2 - Room Three
The dusl on the stone floor shows signs of small
animals, but is otherwise undisturbed. The iron
bound door in the north wall hangs from rusty
hinges. A simple sliding bolt secures it. The
scraping sound can be heard through the floor
and the fragrance of cut wood freshens the air.
