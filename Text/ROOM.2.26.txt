Level 2 - Room Twenty-six
Large vats and tubs are set into the floor of the
small chamber and heaps of rotting clothing litter
the room. The sound of flowing water can be heard
through the moss hung doorway to the east. The
dusty floor is undisturbed and the air smells of
damp greenery.
